Get Our App

The Tesla app puts owners in direct communication with their vehicles and energy products anytime, anywhere. With this app, you can: 1. Check charging progress in real time and start or stop charging 2. Heat or cool your car before driving – even if it's in a garage 3. Lock or unlock from afar, Locate your vehicle with directions or track its movement 4. Send an address from your favorite apps to start navigation in your car 5. Allow your passengers to quickly control media 6. Flash lights or honk the horn to find your vehicle when parked 7. Vent or close the panoramic roof 8. Summon your vehicle out of your garage or a tight parking space (for vehicles with Autopilot 8. Update your vehicle software from wherever you are and Engage with Powerwall: monitor how much energy is stored from solar 9. used by your home, or exported to the grid.
